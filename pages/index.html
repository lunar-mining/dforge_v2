Polytechnology Committee
of North Syria

We are the official body for civil technology development of North Syria. Our objective as the people's technical committee of Rojava is the creation of a socio-economic technological paradigm whereby civil society becomes self-organized with a non-state paradigm. This provides the means for communities and nations to escape the totalizing enclosure of global modernity irregardless of the structure of power. Peoples become self reliant in the spheres of society, economy and politics. The body politic acts as an autonomous whole, with self-defense mechanisms down to every cell. This confederation of communities is able to exist within other political systems, operating on supra-national scales based off its effective use of technology to organize its subjects in furthering the democratic nation paradigm.

Main projects

Self-sufficient Hardware

Our aim is to manufacture our own routers and telephones based off opensource technology. The end goal is that north syria becomes completely self sufficient in every area of technical needs.

Crypto and Blockchain

To develop and popularize decentralized and anonymous crypto-based systems for society, politics and economy. Using advanced modern techniques for p2p self organization.

Autonomous Networks

Construct infrastructure to enable the population to access free services for education and self organization, as well as for local ISPs to compete in selling internet access. The operation of this network is governed under a cooperative contract.

Hacker Academies

Open academies in every major city for the youth to learn modern technologies, engineering, maths and science. They will also be centers for disseminating our philosophy which is the guiding force of our work.

FAQ

What is the project status?
We are in the process of training developers, and deploying infrastructure projects (network, operating system dev, cryptosystems).
What is the role of technology?
With the war in Syria winding down, the emphasis will now shift to a civil society building project. As a landlocked region, with limited access to outside, technology is a multiplier for every area of society, politics and economy. The region is facing serious challenges, and its ability to develop technology is crucial to ensure its survival.
What are your responsibilities?
We are responsible for the civil technical development plans, reviewing proposals to the administration, iniating projects, and training technical specialists.
What support are you looking for?
We are looking for high level specialists able to provide training and expertise, either visiting here or online, partnerships with organizations that have project proposals, and recruitment of developers able to come here for long periods of time and be responsible for projects.
